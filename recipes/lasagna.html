Lasagna

Description

This is a great Italian, cheesy, delicious treat.

    cheese beef lasagna pasta pasta sauce seasonings

    Preheat oven at 350 degrees F. Cook beef until brown. Open box of pasta, place one piece of pasta in cooking pan Pour pasta sauce, beef, and cheese on pasta. Repeat steps 2 and 3 until no nore pasta remains Place pan in oven for 45 min at 325 degrees F.
