Pizza

Description

This is a great Italian, cheesy, delicious treat.

    Yeast Sugar Bread Flour Olive Oil Salt

    Gather all ingredients. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C), and lightly grease a pizza pan. Place warm water in a bowl; add yeast and sugar. Mix and let stand until creamy, about 10 minutes. Add flour, oil, and salt to the yeast mixture; beat until smooth. You can do this by hand or use a stand mixer fitted with a dough hook to make it easier.
